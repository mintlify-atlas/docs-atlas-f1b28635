## Overview

**Finance Monitor** is a specialized variant focused on **global markets, trading intelligence, and financial infrastructure**. It tracks 92 stock exchanges, 19 financial centers, 13 central banks, commodities, forex, crypto, and economic indicators worldwide.

<Info>
  **Live Demo:** [finance.worldmonitor.app](https://finance.worldmonitor.app)
</Info>

---

## Key Features

### Market Coverage

- **92 global stock exchanges** mapped by geography:
  - **Mega markets:** NYSE, NASDAQ, Shanghai Stock Exchange, Euronext, Tokyo Stock Exchange
  - **Major exchanges:** Hong Kong, London, NSE/BSE (India), Toronto, Korea Exchange, Saudi Tadawul
  - **Emerging markets:** São Paulo, Johannesburg, Jakarta, Istanbul, Warsaw, Cairo
- **19 financial centers** ranked by Global Financial Centres Index (New York #1 → offshore centers)
- **13 central banks** (Federal Reserve, ECB, BoJ, BoE, PBoC, SNB, RBA, BoC, RBI, BoK, BCB, SAMA) + supranational institutions (BIS, IMF)
- **10 commodity hubs** (CME Group, ICE, LME, SHFE, DCE, TOCOM, DGCX, MCX, Rotterdam, Houston)

### Finance-Specific Layers

- **Stock exchanges** with market cap, trading hours, and index data
- **Financial centers** with GFCI rank and specialization (banking, insurance, fintech)
- **Central banks** with policy rate data and meeting schedules
- **Commodity hubs** (physical + exchanges) with spot price tracking
- **Gulf FDI investments** — 64 Saudi/UAE foreign direct investments plotted globally, color-coded by status (operational, under-construction, announced), sized by investment amount
- **WTO trade policy intelligence** — active trade restrictions, tariff trends, bilateral trade flows, SPS/TBT barriers

### Market Radar

7-signal macro radar with composite BUY/CASH verdict:

1. **Bitcoin technical trend** (SMA50, SMA200, VWAP, Mayer Multiple)
2. **Fear & Greed Index** with 30-day history
3. **JPY liquidity signal** (USD/JPY rate)
4. **BTC spot ETF flows** (IBIT, FBTC, GBTC, and 7 more)
5. **Stablecoin peg health** (USDT, USDC, DAI, FDUSD, USDe)
6. **QQQ/XLP macro regime** (risk-on vs. risk-off)
7. **Bitcoin hash rate** (network security)

Inline SVG sparklines and donut gauges for visual trends.

### Finance News Feeds (120+)

- **Market news:** Bloomberg, CNBC, MarketWatch, Financial Times, Yahoo Finance
- **Central banks:** Federal Reserve, ECB, Bank of England, BoJ, PBoC
- **Regulatory:** SEC, FINRA, CFTC, FCA, ESMA
- **Economic data:** FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data), EIA, World Bank, IMF
- **Crypto:** CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, The Block
- **Commodities:** OPEC, IEA, CME Group, ICE

## Finance-Specific Features

### Gulf FDI Investment Layer

**64 Saudi/UAE foreign direct investments** plotted globally:

- **Color-coded by status:**
  - Green: Operational
  - Yellow: Under construction
  - Blue: Announced
- **Sized by investment amount:** $100M → $10B+
- **Sectors:** Energy, real estate, infrastructure, tech, healthcare

**Data source:** Public announcements from sovereign wealth funds (PIF, Mubadala, ADQ, ADIA)

### WTO Trade Policy Intelligence

Tracks trade restrictions and barriers from the World Trade Organization:

- **Active trade restrictions** (tariffs, quotas, embargoes)
- **Tariff trends** (average applied rates by country)
- **Bilateral trade flows** (top import/export partners)
- **SPS/TBT barriers** (sanitary/phytosanitary and technical barriers to trade)

**Data source:** WTO API (requires `WTO_API_KEY` in runtime config)

### BIS Central Bank Data

Bank for International Settlements data integration:

- **Policy rates** across major economies (updated monthly)
- **Real effective exchange rates (REER)** for 60+ currencies
- **Credit-to-GDP ratios** (financial stability indicator)

**Code reference:** `src/services/economic/index.ts` (BIS data handler)

### Market Radar Composite Signal

The 7-signal radar produces a **BUY/CASH verdict** using weighted scoring:

```typescript
const signals = [
  { name: 'BTC Trend', weight: 0.20, value: btcTrendScore },
  { name: 'Fear & Greed', weight: 0.15, value: fearGreedScore },
  { name: 'JPY Liquidity', weight: 0.10, value: jpyScore },
  { name: 'ETF Flows', weight: 0.15, value: etfFlowScore },
  { name: 'Stablecoin Peg', weight: 0.15, value: stablecoinScore },
  { name: 'QQQ/XLP', weight: 0.15, value: qqQxlpScore },
  { name: 'Hash Rate', weight: 0.10, value: hashRateScore },
];

const composite = signals.reduce((sum, s) => sum + s.value * s.weight, 0);
const verdict = composite > 0.6 ? 'BUY' : composite < 0.4 ? 'CASH' : 'NEUTRAL';
```

## Use Cases

### Traders

- **Multi-asset correlation:** Track crypto, stocks, commodities, and forex in one view
- **Macro regime detection:** QQQ/XLP ratio signals risk-on vs. risk-off
- **Central bank calendar:** Policy rate decisions and meeting schedules

### Institutional Investors

- **Emerging market coverage:** 92 exchanges including frontier markets (Cairo, Lagos, Nairobi)
- **Sovereign wealth fund tracking:** Gulf FDI layer shows PIF/Mubadala/ADQ investments
- **Trade policy risk:** WTO trade restrictions impact supply chain and tariff exposure

### Economists

- **BIS data access:** Policy rates, REER, and credit-to-GDP ratios in one dashboard
- **Economic indicator alerts:** FRED integration for unemployment, GDP, CPI, inflation
- **Commodity price tracking:** Oil (WTI, Brent), metals (gold, copper, lithium), agricultural

### Crypto Analysts

- **ETF flow tracker:** IBIT, FBTC, GBTC inflows/outflows (daily updates)
- **Stablecoin peg health:** USDT, USDC, DAI, FDUSD, USDe deviation from $1.00
- **Hash rate monitoring:** Bitcoin network security proxy
- **On-chain metrics:** Integration with Dune Analytics, Glassnode, CryptoQuant
